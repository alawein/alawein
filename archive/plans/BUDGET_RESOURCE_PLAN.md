### **High-Risk Areas**

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|-------------|--------|-------------------|
| **Scope Creep** | Medium | High | Fixed project scope, change request process |
| **Technical Complexity** | Medium | Medium | Expert consultation, phased development |
| **Content Delays** | High | Medium | Parallel content creation, templates |
| **Third-party Costs** | Low | Medium | Fixed-price contracts, contingency fund |

1. **Open Source First**: Maximize use of free/open-source technologies
2. **Shared Infrastructure**: Reuse components and services across platforms
3. **Phased Development**: Deploy incrementally to manage cash flow
4. **Family Contributions**: Leverage family skills for content and testing
5. **Long-term Contracts**: Negotiate annual rates for better pricing
