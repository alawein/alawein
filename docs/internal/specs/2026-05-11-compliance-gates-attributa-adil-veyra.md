## 1 · attributa

Catalog posture noted **internal-only** compliance property while outward narrative invokes trust and attribution themes; reconcile before amplification.

### Schedule checklist

1. **`Schedule` Claims audit session (60 minutes):** Enumerate assertions on homepage, README, APIs, demos; map each to reproducible artifact or downgrade language.
2. **`Schedule` Data handling narrative review (45 minutes):** Document retention boundaries for uploads, logs, and model scoring; privacy policy alignment.
3. **`Schedule` Security marketing review (45 minutes):** Third-party wording on CWE-like framing versus actual checks implemented; forbid absolute guarantees on detector certainty if models are stochastic.
4. **Exit gate:** Steward signs **Ready for selective outbound on agreed ICP**.

### Primary risks echoed from discovery

Detector false-positive brand harm; jurisdictional churn on AI-labelled content rules.

## 2 · adil

Catalog flags **regulated** posture; outbound must assume **heightened scrutiny** analogous to regulated professional workflows even when positioning as tooling-only.

### Schedule checklist

1. **`Schedule` Jurisdiction-scope counsel review (minimum one session plus written memo):** Confirm acceptable software-only positioning; disclaimers placement; escalation path for barred practice optics.

3. **`Schedule` Security architecture summary for SOC2-aligned prospects:** Separate calendar only if deliberate enterprise lane; optional otherwise until pilot revenue signal.
4. **Exit gate:** Written **risk memo** archived with link in project SSOT (`SSOT.md` or repo-local equivalent for that product repo) before prospect pilots.

## 3 · veyra *(conditional outbound)*

Workspace visibility policy emphasizes **employer clearance** uncertainty; treat outbound as gated until stewardship confirms written clearance posture.

### Schedule checklist

1. **`Schedule` Ownership and public-storyline checkpoint (product steward plus legal liaison):** Employer or client-bound constraints enumerated; permissible public problem statements listed explicitly.
2. **`Schedule` Biometric-sensitive data review (privacy counsel or qualified advisor):** Voice retention durations; regional sensitivities beyond generic GDPR wording.
3. **`Schedule` Technical claims alignment (engineering plus product narrative):** Map latency, accuracy, and governance promises to demos that can replay under NDA without synthetic embellishment.
4. **Exit gate:** Documented **`visibility: conditional cleared`** state with reviewer initials; until then prohibit broad cold outbound and conference talks positioned as merchantable product truths.
