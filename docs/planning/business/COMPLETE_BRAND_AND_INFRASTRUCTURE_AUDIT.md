### When to Form Each LLC

| LLC                          | Formation Trigger        | Rationale                            |
| ---------------------------- | ------------------------ | ------------------------------------ |
| **Alawein Technologies LLC** | NOW                      | IP protection for TalAI, Librex      |
| **REPZ LLC**                 | 10K users OR $5K revenue | Liability protection for fitness app |
| **Live It Iconic LLC**       | First sale               | E-commerce liability                 |

### Formation Decision Matrix

```
Revenue < $1K/year ‚Üí Sole Proprietorship (no LLC needed)
Revenue $1K-$10K ‚Üí Single LLC (Alawein Technologies)
Revenue > $10K per product line ‚Üí Separate LLCs
Employees or contractors ‚Üí LLC required
```

### Recommended Action

1. **Form Alawein Technologies LLC (California)** - $70 now, ~$800/year
2. **Delay REPZ LLC** until app has traction
3. **Delay Live It Iconic LLC** until first e-commerce sale
