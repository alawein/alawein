# Brand copy guidelines

This file is a quick-reference projection of [`VOICE.md`](VOICE.md).
Use `VOICE.md` as the canonical brand-copy authority.

## Brand posture

The Alawein brand reads as rigorous, modern, and grounded. Copy should communicate craft, verification, and technical clarity without sounding sterile.

## Rules

- Prefer factual product names and category labels.
- Keep product copy compatible with repo reality.
- Match the repo lifecycle in the copy. Do not present planned work as shipped.
- Align naming with the terminology registry.

## Allowed variation

- Product repos may use sharper benefit language.
- Research repos may use more abstract or editorial framing.
- Tooling repos should remain utilitarian and explicit.

## Disallowed variation

- Taglines that contradict lifecycle or visibility.
- Claims of automation, scale, or security without evidence in the repo.
- Copy that obscures ownership, maintenance status, or deployment state.
