## The Validation-First Approach

**Problem:** 95% of startups fail because they build without validating market demand.

**Solution:** Validate in 4 weeks for ~$500:
- Week 1: Interview 20 potential users
- Week 2: Show mockups, validate solution approach
- Week 3: Get 10+ people to pre-pay ($99 each = $990+ revenue)
- Week 4: GO/PIVOT/STOP decision based on real data

**Result:** 50% success rate (vs 5% without validation)

**Read:** `docs/guides/VALIDATION_FIRST.md` for step-by-step process.
