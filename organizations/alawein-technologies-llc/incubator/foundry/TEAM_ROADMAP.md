# Team Roadmap & Collaboration

**How to organize, coordinate, and scale your team through the Foundry framework.**

For teams building together (not solopreneur track).

---

## ðŸŽ¯ Team Structure by Stage

### Phase 0-1: Validation (Solo or Pair)

**Team size:** 1-2 people

**Roles needed:**
- **Founder/Validator** - Talks to customers, owns validation
- **(Optional) Co-founder** - Supports validation, handles logistics

**Responsibilities:**
- Founder: 20 hours/week on customer interviews
- Co-founder: Logistics, scheduling, note-taking, analysis

**Communication:** Weekly sync (1 hour)

**Tools needed:**
- Spreadsheet (tracking interviews)
- Calendar (scheduling)
- Slack/Discord (async communication)
- Google Drive (shared docs)

---

### Phase 2: MVP Build (Small Team)

**Team size:** 2-4 people

**Roles needed:**
- **Founder/Product** - Owns product vision, customer communication
- **Lead Engineer** - Technical lead, architecture decisions
- **Designer** (optional) - UI/UX, visual design
- **Project Manager** (optional) - Timeline tracking, coordination

**Responsibilities:**
- Founder: 40 hours/week on product, customer support
- Engineer(s): 40-50 hours/week building features
- Designer: 20-30 hours/week on UI/UX
- PM: 10-15 hours/week coordinating

**Communication:** Daily standup (15 min) + Weekly planning (1 hour)

**Tools needed:**
- GitHub (code management)
- Figma (design)
- Linear/Jira (task tracking)
- Slack (communication)
- Notion/Google Docs (documentation)

---

### Phase 3: Launch (Growing Team)

**Team size:** 4-8 people

**Roles needed:**
- **Founder/CEO** - Vision, fundraising, strategy
- **VP Engineering** - Tech lead, hiring
- **VP Product** - Roadmap, customer communication
- **VP Marketing** - Growth, content, partnerships
- **Designers** (2) - Product & marketing design
- **Engineers** (2-3) - Feature development, infrastructure
- **Operations** (1) - Finance, HR, legal

**Responsibilities:**
- Founder: Fundraising, partnerships, strategy (30 hours/week)
- VP Eng: Hiring, architecture, releases (40 hours/week)
- VP Product: Roadmap, customer research (40 hours/week)
- VP Marketing: Growth, launches (40 hours/week)
- Others: Full-time in their domains

**Communication:**
- Daily: Standup (15 min) + Async Slack updates
- Weekly: Full team sync (1 hour)
- Weekly: Department syncs (30-45 min each)
- Bi-weekly: 1-on-1s

**Tools needed:**
- GitHub (code)
- Figma (design)
- Linear (tasks)
- Slack (communication)
- Notion (documentation)
- Google Workspace (documents)
- Loom (async video updates)

---

### Phase 4: Scaling (Mature Team)

**Team size:** 8-20+ people

**Departments:**
- Engineering (5-10)
- Product (2-3)
- Design (2-3)
- Marketing/Growth (2-3)
- Operations (2-3)
- Customer Success (2-3)

**New roles:**
- Engineering Manager (for 5+ engineers)
- Hiring Manager
- Customer Success Manager
- Content Manager
- Data Analyst

**Communication structure:**
- Daily: Standup (20 min)
- Weekly: Department syncs (1 hour each)
- Weekly: Leadership team (1 hour)
- Bi-weekly: All-hands (1 hour)
- Monthly: Strategy planning (2 hours)

**Management overhead:** ~20% of team time

## ðŸš€ Hiring Guide

### When to Hire Each Role

**Phase 2 (MVP Build)**
- First hire: Lead Engineer (if founder isn't technical)
- Second hire: Designer (or outsource initially)

**Phase 3 (Launch)**
- Engineers (2-3 more)
- Product Manager (if founder needs support)
- Marketing Operator

**Phase 4 (Scale)**
- VP hires (Eng, Product, Marketing)
- Department expansion
- Specialized roles (data, ops, etc.)

---

### Hiring Process Template

1. **Define role** - Write job description from [relevant template]
2. **Post & source** - LinkedIn, Twitter, referrals, job boards
3. **Screen resumes** - Look for [specific criteria]
4. **Phone screen** - 30 min call with hiring manager
5. **Technical test** (if applicable) - Real-world task
6. **In-depth interview** - 1 hour with hiring manager
7. **Team interviews** - 30 min each with 2-3 team members
8. **Reference calls** - Talk to 2 previous managers
9. **Offer & negotiate** - Written offer, discussion
10. **Onboarding** - Follow ONBOARDING.md

## ðŸ“Š Team Health Metrics

Track monthly:

- **Turnover rate** (goal: <10% annually)
- **Happiness score** (goal: >8/10)
- **Retention rate** (goal: >90%)
- **Team growth rate** (planned vs actual)
- **Hiring cost per role** (budget tracking)
- **Time to productivity** (how long to first contribution)
- **Code review turnaround** (goal: <4 hours)
- **Blocker resolution time** (goal: <1 day)

## ðŸ¤ Communication Norms

### How We Communicate

- **Urgent (15 min response):** Direct message @person
- **Important (4 hr response):** #team-channel message
- **Regular (1 day response):** Email or long-form doc
- **Async:** Slack threads, Google Docs comments
- **Sync:** Scheduled meetings only

### Slack Channels

- `#general` - Company announcements
- `#engineering` - Engineering team
- `#product` - Product discussions
- `#marketing` - Marketing team
- `#random` - Off-topic
- `#wins` - Celebrate successes
- `#help` - Ask for help
- `#ideas` - New ideas discussion
