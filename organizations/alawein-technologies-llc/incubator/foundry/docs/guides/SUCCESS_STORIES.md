### Example 2: Small Team MVP Build

```markdown
## CHAOS ENGINE - 12 Week MVP

**When:** February-April 2024
**Stage:** MVP Build
**Team Size:** 3 people (founder + engineer + designer)

### The Challenge
Had validated the idea (10 pre-sales), now needed to build MVP in 12 weeks with small team.

### What You Built
- Hypothesis collision engine backend (Python/FastAPI)
- Web interface for generating hypotheses
- Database of cross-domain examples
- Scoring system for hypothesis quality
- Basic analytics

### Results
- Shipped on time (week 12)
- All 10 pre-sale customers using it
- 50% of them still using after first month
- First customer feedback: "Game-changing"
- First bug reports: 3 fixable issues in Week 1

### Key Learnings
1. MVP didn't need all 10 planned features - 4 features was enough
2. Shipping early > building perfect
3. First customers are patient but honest
4. Weekly demo to pre-sales was critical feedback loop
5. Remote team worked great with daily standups

### Tools/Resources That Helped
- IMPLEMENTATION_GUIDE.md (the build playbook)
- PHASE_1_QUICK_WINS.md (week-by-week breakdown)
- GETTING_STARTED.md (dev setup saved hours)
- EXECUTION_CHECKLISTS.md (Phase 2 checklist kept us on track)
- TEAM_ROADMAP.md (managed team communication well)

### Tips for Others
- Start with most core feature only
- Demo to real customers weekly
- Don't over-engineer - remember it's MVP
- Keep pre-sales close - they're your best testers
- Celebrate shipping! It's huge!

### Contact
[Link to team/company]
```

### Example 3: From Launch to $5K MRR

```markdown
## RESEARCH PRISON - 8-Week Launch to $5K MRR

**When:** May-June 2024
**Stage:** Public Launch
**Team Size:** 2 people

### The Challenge
Had validated + built MVP. Now needed to get from 10 customers to 50-100 and reach $5K MRR.

### What You Built
- Product Hunt launch (ranked #5)
- Landing page redesign
- 3 blog posts about validation methodology
- Email nurture sequence
- Twitter content calendar
- Free tier with upgrade path

### Results
- Product Hunt: 200 upvotes, #5 of day
- Website: 2,000 visitors Week 1
- Signups: 100+ Week 1
- Paid customers: 45 by end of Week 2
- MRR: $5,100 (exceeded $5K target!)
- Retention: 85% after 2 weeks

### Key Learnings
1. Product Hunt is insanely valuable for initial launch momentum
2. Free tier converts 5-8% to paid (lower than we hoped)
3. Email from PH launch list is goldmine - stay in touch
4. Product differentiation wins over marketing spend
5. Customer testimonials more important than our claims

### Tools/Resources That Helped
- MARKETING_PLAYBOOK.md (all templates we used)
- METRICS_DASHBOARD.md (tracked all metrics)
- METRICS_SETUP_GUIDE.md (set up proper analytics)
- DECISION_MATRIX.md (pricing decision was critical)
- templates/email/ (email sequences)

### Tips for Others
- Start free tier marketing before launch
- Get 50+ PH upvotes before day 1 launch (tell your network)
- Respond to EVERY comment in first 12 hours
- Email past customers/interview people - they're your launch army
- Have support ready - customer inquiries = good problem
- Celebrate but stay focused - 45 customers is start, not finish

### Contact
[Link to company]
```
