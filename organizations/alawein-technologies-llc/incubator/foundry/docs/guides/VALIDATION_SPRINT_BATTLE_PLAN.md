# ‚ö†Ô∏è WHAT COULD GO WRONG

**Problem: Can't get people on calls**
- Solution: Use LinkedIn, ask for referrals aggressively, offer different time slots
- Fallback: Do async text interviews (send script, get written responses)

**Problem: People not interested in any idea**
- Solution: Maybe it's the pitch angle. Pivot the concept. Test different customer segment.
- Fallback: This is still valuable learning - it's a "validated invalid" idea

**Problem: All three ideas look equally good**
- Solution: Go deeper into one. Pick winner by:
  - Largest market size (how many potential customers?)
  - Best price signal (who'd pay most?)
  - Most customer enthusiasm (gut feel)

**Problem: No clear pattern after 15 interviews**
- Solution: Interview 5 more (total 20). Keep going until signal is clear.
