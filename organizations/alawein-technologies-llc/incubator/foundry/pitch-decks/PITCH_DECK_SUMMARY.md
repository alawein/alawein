## INVESTMENT THESIS COMPARISON

### RESEARCH PRISON - "The Safest Bet"
**Why Invest:**
- Clearest value prop: Save 6 months of wasted research
- Lowest technical risk (simplest to build)
- Fastest to revenue (Month 1)
- Most defensible (proprietary question bank)

**Investment Highlights:**
- $1.5M gets to profitability
- 68% of researchers have immediate pain point
- Clear before/after metric (hypothesis survival)
- Academic credentials of founder critical

**Primary Risk:** Market education needed

---

### NIGHTMARE MODE - "The Viral Rocket"
**Why Invest:**
- Highest viral potential (gamification + public battles)
- Largest revenue opportunity (Year 1: $2M)
- Strongest network effects (leaderboards, tournaments)
- Best retention metrics (92% 30-day)

**Investment Highlights:**
- $2.5M builds category-defining brand
- Product-market fit proven in beta (NPS: 83)
- Multiple revenue streams (battles, certificates, tournaments)
- Press loves the story

**Primary Risk:** Could be "too brutal" for some

---

### CHAOS ENGINE - "The Moonshot"
**Why Invest:**
- Largest TAM ($24.3B innovation market)
- Highest LTV:CAC (6.3:1)
- Platform potential (marketplace, API, ecosystem)
- Most differentiated (no competition)

**Investment Highlights:**
- $2M creates new category
- Enterprise contracts worth $36K/year
- Patents create defensive moat
- Exit potential to consultancies

**Primary Risk:** Harder to prove ROI initially
