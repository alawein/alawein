## üí° KEY INSIGHTS

### Research Prison
- **Best For:** Risk-averse investors seeking quick profitability
- **Hook:** "6 months into your PhD, you discover your hypothesis is doomed"
- **Key Metric:** 89% of users find critical flaws they missed
- **Viral Mechanism:** "Watch My Hypothesis Die" videos

### Nightmare Mode
- **Best For:** Growth investors seeking viral rocket ship
- **Hook:** "After 3 years of research, your paper gets desk-rejected in 48 hours"
- **Key Metric:** Papers scoring >70% have 3.2x higher acceptance rate
- **Viral Mechanism:** Public paper battles with spectator mode

### Chaos Engine
- **Best For:** Vision-driven investors seeking category creation
- **Hook:** "What if antibiotics came from colliding mold with warfare?"
- **Key Metric:** 1 in 100 ideas rated "breakthrough potential"
- **Viral Mechanism:** "Weirdest Working Combination" challenges
