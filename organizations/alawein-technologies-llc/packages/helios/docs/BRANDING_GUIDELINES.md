## Brand Essence

### Mission
To democratize autonomous research by making hypothesis generation, validation, and learning accessible to everyone.

A world where AI and humans collaborate seamlessly to accelerate scientific discovery across all domains.

### Values
- **Innovation**: Pushing boundaries in autonomous research
- **Integrity**: Rigorous validation and ethical practices
- **Inclusivity**: Accessible to researchers of all backgrounds
- **Excellence**: Production-grade systems, not prototypes

## Writing Style

### Brand Voice

**Tone**: Professional yet approachable, confident but not arrogant

**Characteristics**:
- Clear and direct
- Avoid jargon (or explain when necessary)
- Use active voice
- Emphasize benefits, not features
- Inclusive language

### Writing Guidelines

âœ… **Do**
- "HELIOS makes research faster"
- "64 production-ready algorithms"
- "Researchers of all backgrounds"
- "Validated hypotheses save time"
- "Start in 5 minutes"

âŒ **Don't**
- "HELIOS synergizes AI paradigms"
- "Proprietary algorithm implementations"
- "For experts only"
- "Advanced users can leverage..."
- "Complicated multi-step process"

### Taglines

**Primary**: "Autonomous Research Discovery"
**Secondary**: "From hypothesis to insight"
**Tertiary**: "Making research intelligent"

## Accessibility

### Color Contrast

- Text on colored backgrounds: 4.5:1 minimum
- Graphics on colored backgrounds: 3:1 minimum
- Logo on photo: 7:1 for readability

### Text Alternatives

- All images: `alt` text describing content
- Logo: Alternative text: "HELIOS logo"
- Icons: Described in context or with aria-label

### Motion

- Animations: Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`
- Transitions: Keep under 300ms for clarity
- Avoid: Flashing, strobing (> 3 times per second)
