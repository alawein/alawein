# Hiring Guide: Join the Librex.QAP-new Team

Comprehensive job descriptions and recruitment strategy for growing the Librex.QAP-new community.

---

## Overview

Librex.QAP-new is seeking talented engineers and researchers to join our mission of advancing combinatorial optimization. We're building the bridge between quantum computing and machine learning communities.

---

## Role 1: Optimization Specialist

### Position Summary

We're looking for an experienced optimization researcher to lead algorithm development and benchmarking for Librex.QAP-new.

### Responsibilities

- **Algorithm Implementation**: Develop and implement novel optimization methods (quantum-inspired, classical, hybrid)
- **Benchmarking**: Design and execute comprehensive performance benchmarks across problem instances
- **Method Validation**: Prove correctness and measure quality of new algorithms
- **Documentation**: Write detailed algorithm descriptions and mathematical proofs
- **Community**: Present research at conferences and publish in peer-reviewed venues

### Required Qualifications

- **Education**: MS/PhD in Computer Science, Operations Research, Mathematics, or Physics
- **Experience**: 3+ years with optimization algorithms
- **Skills**:
  - Strong mathematical foundation (linear algebra, optimization theory)
  - Python proficiency
  - Familiarity with QAP or similar combinatorial problems
  - Git and GitHub workflow

### Preferred Qualifications

- Publications in optimization/ML conferences (ICML, ICLR, AAAI, OR venues)
- Experience with quantum computing concepts
- Open source contribution history
- Machine learning background

### Compensation & Benefits

- Competitive salary commensurate with experience
- Remote-first opportunity
- Flexible hours
- Conference travel budget
- Publication support
- Equity/revenue sharing (depending on arrangement)

### Hiring Process

1. Initial conversation (30 mins)
2. Technical screening on optimization theory (60 mins)
3. Algorithm implementation task (take-home, 3 days)
4. Final discussion with leadership (45 mins)
