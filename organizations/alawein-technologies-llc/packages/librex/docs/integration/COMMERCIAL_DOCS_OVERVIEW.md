- **Status**: Filed October 2025
- **Claims**: 26 total (15 method + 8 system + 3 software)
- **Innovation**: FFT preconditioning of optimization gradients
- **Market**: $100B+ optimization market
- **Advantage**: 50-500x speedup over existing methods

### Patent Family 2: Quantum Gradient Preconditioning ⏳
- **Status**: Ready for filing (January 2026)
- **Claims**: 24 total covering quantum preconditioning methods
- **Innovation**: Electron correlation effects in optimization preconditioning
- **Market**: $200B+ pharmaceutical and biotech market
- **Advantage**: Quantum accuracy with polynomial scaling vs exponential DFT

### Patent Family 3: ML-Trained Attractor Agents ⏳
- **Status**: Ready for filing (February 2026)
- **Claims**: 22 total covering ML control of dynamical systems
- **Innovation**: RL control of attractor programming with theoretical guarantees
- **Market**: $100B+ optimization and AI market
- **Advantage**: Theoretical guarantees with learned optimization strategies
