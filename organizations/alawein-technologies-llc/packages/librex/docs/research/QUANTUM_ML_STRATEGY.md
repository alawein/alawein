## Executive Summary

Librex.QAP-new is uniquely positioned as a **bridge technology** between quantum computing and machine learning by focusing on **combinatorial optimization via continuous relaxation**. This document outlines strategic positioning, community engagement, and partnership opportunities.

### Key Insight
While quantum computers promise speedups for combinatorial optimization, they're 5-10 years from practical advantage. Librex.QAP-new offers:
- **For ML Community:** Advanced optimization methods that enhance neural networks and learning
- **For Quantum Community:** A testbed for hybrid classical-quantum algorithms
- **For Both:** A foundation for next-generation optimization
