## **2. "FAILURE FUTURES MARKET" - Bet on Which Ideas Will Fail**

### **The Crazy Idea**
Create a prediction market where people bet fake currency on whether hypotheses will be validated or refuted. The Hall of Failures becomes a trading floor.

### **How It Works**
- **Each hypothesis gets a futures contract**: 0-100% chance of validation
- **Users trade "Failure Tokens"**: Buy low on ideas you think will fail, sell high when they're disproven
- **Wisdom of Crowds**: Market consensus predicts which research directions are promising
- **AI Training Data**: Use market prices to train better hypothesis generators
- **Quarterly Settlement**: When experiments complete, contracts pay out
- **Leaderboard**: Top predictors get "Oracle" badges

### **Why It's Insane**
Turns scientific skepticism into a game. Harnesses collective intelligence to prioritize research. Could be more accurate than peer review.

### **Integration**
- Feed market prices back into ORCHEX to adjust hypothesis prioritization
- Give "Oracle" badge holders voting power on platform governance
- Allow researchers to "hedge" their own hypotheses (prove you're genuinely uncertain)
