# Brand Guidelines

Essence
- Balance (mīzān): fairness, stability, and measured trade-offs.
- Clarity: precise claims, verifiable metrics, no hype.

Voice & Tone
- Direct, technical, and actionable. Avoid emojis and superlatives.

Visual Direction (suggestions)
- Simple scale/balance motif. Neutral palette with one accent color.
- Prefer legible monospace/code blocks for technical content.

Terminology
- MEZAN (umbrella), ORCHEX (research engine), TURING (product engine), Libria Suite (solver family), *Flow names for displays.
