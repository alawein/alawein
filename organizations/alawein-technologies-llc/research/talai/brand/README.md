## Quick Reference

**Main Brand Document:** [BRAND_IDENTITY.md](../BRAND_IDENTITY.md)

**Logo Concept:**
- Cartoon Talal with wizard hat and robotic AI eyes
- Mischievous smile
- Dew drops floating around
- Color: Blue + Purple + Gold

**Mascot Poses Needed:**
1. Default (smiling, confident)
2. Thinking (pondering)
3. Celebrating (success)
4. Confused (errors, but cute)
5. Waving (welcome)

## For Designers

See [BRAND_IDENTITY.md](../BRAND_IDENTITY.md) section "Design Brief for Artists" for complete specifications.

**Key Requirements:**
- Modern cartoon style (Pixar/Ghibli fusion)
- Robotic eyes (glowing, circuit-like, warm)
- Wizard touch (small hat or cape, not too serious)
- Middle Eastern features (honoring Saudi heritage)
- **The smile is key** - mischievous, knowing, playful
