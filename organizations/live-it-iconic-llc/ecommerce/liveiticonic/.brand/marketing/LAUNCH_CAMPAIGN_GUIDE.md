## Campaign Overview

### Goals

**Primary Goal:** Launch YouTube channel with 500+ subscribers in first 30 days

**Secondary Goals:**
- 5,000+ total views across all videos
- 500+ Instagram followers
- 200+ email subscribers
- Establish brand recognition in automotive community

### Strategy

**Phase 1: Build Anticipation (Pre-Launch)**
- Tease content without revealing everything
- Build email list
- Create FOMO (fear of missing out)

**Phase 2: Launch with Impact (Launch Day)**
- Release 6-8 videos simultaneously (content library)
- Coordinate cross-platform announcement
- Drive traffic from all channels to YouTube

**Phase 3: Sustain Momentum (Post-Launch)**
- Consistent publishing schedule
- Engagement with community
- Leverage early wins to attract more viewers

## Paid Advertising (Optional)

### When to Use Paid Ads

**Consider paid ads if:**
- You have budget ($500-1,000 to test)
- Organic growth is slower than expected
- You want to accelerate subscriber growth

**Skip paid ads if:**
- Budget is tight (organic can work)
- Content isn't validated yet (test organically first)

---

### Facebook/Instagram Ads

**Objective:** Video Views (Drive traffic to YouTube)

**Targeting:**
- Interests: Cars, automotive, specific car brands, Hagerty, Petrolicious, similar channels
- Age: 25-45
- Gender: All (though likely skews male)
- Location: US, Canada, UK, Australia

**Creative:**
- Use best-performing thumbnail as ad image
- 30-second teaser video (vertical for Instagram/Facebook Stories)
- Headline: "Automotive Storytelling, Authentically Told"
- CTA: "Watch on YouTube"

**Budget:**
- Start with $10-20/day for 7 days ($70-140 total)
- Monitor CPM (cost per 1,000 impressions) and CTR (click-through rate)
- If CTR > 2%, continue. If < 1%, pause and revise creative.

---

### YouTube Ads (Discovery Ads)

**Objective:** Drive subscribers

**Targeting:**
- Keywords: "car review", "car vlog", "automotive", "car collection"
- Channels: TheStraightPipes, Savagegeese, Hagerty, etc.
- Videos: Specific videos from competitor channels

**Creative:**
- Use thumbnail + title
- Let YouTube show as "Suggested Video"

**Budget:**
- $5-10/day
- Aim for <$1 per subscriber (if higher, pause and optimize)

## Contingency Plans

### If Growth is Slower Than Expected

**Possible Issues:**

1. **Thumbnails aren't compelling**
   - Solution: A/B test 2-3 new thumbnails, analyze CTR

2. **Titles aren't optimized**
   - Solution: Use TubeBuddy or VidIQ to research keywords

3. **Content isn't resonating**
   - Solution: Ask audience directly (polls, comments), adjust format

4. **Not enough promotion**
   - Solution: Increase social media activity, reach out to guests to share

5. **Algorithm isn't picking it up**
   - Solution: Post YouTube Shorts daily (algorithm boost), increase upload frequency

---

### If Growth is Faster Than Expected

**Opportunities:**

1. **Monetize early**
   - Open merch store sooner (if demand is there)
   - Reach out to sponsors proactively

2. **Scale content production**
   - Film more interviews (build backlog)
   - Hire editor (free up your time)

3. **Engage community deeper**
   - Start membership tier on YouTube
   - Create Patreon for super fans
