## ðŸŽ¬ Example First Interview

**Format (keep it simple):**

**Intro (2 min)**
"Hey, I'm [name]. Welcome to Live It Iconic. We interview interesting people about cars, culture, and living well. Today I'm talking to [guest]..."

**Story (10 min)**
"So [guest], what's your story? How'd you get into [cars/this lifestyle]?"

**Deep Dive (10 min)**
Ask 3-5 follow-up questions. Let them talk. Listen more than you speak.

**Product Moment (2 min)**
"Before we wrap, I want to show you what we're building..."
(Show a Live It Iconic product)
"Link is in the description if you want one"

**Outro (1 min)**
"Thanks [guest]. Subscribe if you liked this. See you next week."

**Total: 25 minutes**

## ðŸ“ž Common Questions

**Q: Should we do both YouTube and Twitch?**
A: YouTube is primary (more discovery). Twitch is bonus (better engagement). Do YouTube consistently, add Twitch when ready.

**Q: How much equipment do we need?**
A: Phone + ring light + USB mic = $150 total. Upgrade later.

**Q: When do we make money?**
A: Month 4-6 from YouTube ads. Month 3 from merch (if you push). Month 6+ from sponsorships.

**Q: What if the first video gets 0 views?**
A: Normal. Share it with friends/network. By video 10 you'll have momentum.

**Q: Should we do daily posts?**
A: No. 2-3x per week is sustainable. Consistency > frequency.

**Q: Do we need fancy thumbnails?**
A: Yes. Thumbnails are 30% of clicks. Invest here.

**Q: When do we hire help?**
A: Month 6 when you're drowning. Editor first, then admin.

**Q: Is this going to work?**
A: If you stay consistent for 6 months and the community responds, yes. The model is proven. The bottleneck is execution.
