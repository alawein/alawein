# YouTube Channel Strategy - Live It Iconic

## Overview
A lifestyle content channel featuring interviews, car culture, luxury living, and the people who embody the "Iconic" lifestyle.

**Channel Goals:**
- 10K subscribers by Month 6
- 100K views per month by Month 3
- Position as authority in automotive lifestyle storytelling
- Drive traffic to website and merchandise sales
- Create shareable, interview-driven content that feels authentic

---

## Content Pillars (Upload 2x per week)

### 1. **INTERVIEWS** (60% of content)
Real conversations with interesting people in your network.

**Format:** 15-30 minutes
**Frequency:** 2x per week
**Structure:**
1. Intro (30 sec) - Who are we talking to?
2. Story/Background (5 min) - Where they're from, how they got here
3. Deep Dive (15 min) - Their passion, philosophy, mistakes, wins
4. The Iconic Question (3 min) - "What does living iconic mean to you?"
5. Outro with product integration (1 min) - Show a Live It Iconic item

**Guest Ideas (Month 1-2):**
- Your friend/business partner
- Local car collector or enthusiast
- Fashion/lifestyle person you know
- Someone who's built something interesting
- Friend of a friend with a cool story

**Logistics:**
- Record on phone (iPhone 15+ or decent Android)
- Find quiet location (garage, office, lounge)
- Good lighting (window light + basic ring light = $40)
- Basic audio: phone mic or $50 USB condenser mic
- Use Riverside.fm (free plan) for synced video + audio recording

### 2. **Car Content** (25% of content)
Showcasing vehicles, ownership experiences, garage tours.

**Possible Formats:**
- "This $500K car costs HOW MUCH to own?" (Research)
- Garage tour videos (friend's collection)
- Car meet event coverage (local)
- "Supercars in the city" (vlog-style)
- Owner interviews (car + person combo)

**Length:** 10-20 minutes
**Equipment:** Phone camera + basic gimbal ($30-50)

### 3. **Lifestyle & Fashion** (10% of content)
Your merchandise, styling tips, brand updates.

**Content Ideas:**
- "How to wear the Iconic collection" (styling video)
- Live It Iconic product reveal videos
- Unboxing/quality deep-dives
- "What I wear when..." (styling for different occasions)
- Behind-the-scenes of product shoots

**Length:** 8-15 minutes

### 4. **Shorts** (Repurpose, don't create separate)
Take best moments from full videos and cut into 60-second clips.

**Best Sources:**
- Funniest interview moments
- Car content highlights
- Fashion tips
- "Did you know?" facts about cars/lifestyle

## Interview Workflow

### Pre-Production
1. **Book Guest** (2 weeks before)
   - Send casual message explaining the vibe
   - Share previous interviews so they know style
   - Pick a date that works for both

2. **Prep Call** (1 week before)
   - 15-min call about topics to explore
   - Not scripted, but topics/talking points
   - Share location/time/equipment info

3. **Location Scout** (3 days before)
   - Test lighting and audio
   - Plan camera angle
   - Have backup location if needed

### Production Day
- **Setup:** 30 minutes
- **Interview:** 30-45 minutes (we'll cut to 20-25 min)
- **Outro/Wrap:** 10 minutes
- **Pack:** 15 minutes

**Equipment Checklist:**
- [ ] Phone + stands/tripod
- [ ] Audio recorder (or Riverside.fm on backup phone)
- [ ] Light (ring light or position near window)
- [ ] Lavalier mic ($15-30 backup)
- [ ] Chargers for everything
- [ ] Release form (simple Google Form)

### Post-Production
1. **Import footage** (same day)
2. **Transcribe** (use Riverside.fm auto transcription)
3. **Rough edit** (1-2 hours)
   - Cut out long pauses, "ums"
   - Add b-roll or title cards
   - Trim to 20-25 minutes
4. **Color grade** (basic - just punch up colors slightly)
5. **Add captions** (download Riverside transcript, add to video)
6. **Create thumbnail** (Canva, 1 min) - Your face/guest face + bold text
7. **Write description** (include guest Instagram, links, timestamps)
8. **Upload & schedule** (publish Thu 6 PM or schedule for optimal time)

**Tools You Need:**
- Riverside.fm (free plan) - recording
- CapCut - editing (free)
- Canva Pro - thumbnails ($13/mo)
- Adobe Premiere or DaVinci Resolve (free version) - color grade

## Thumbnail & Title Strategy

**Thumbnails:** High contrast, guest face + 1-2 words max
- "INTERVIEW: The Collector"
- "$500K CAR Owner"
- "Luxury Explained"

**Titles:** Hook + curiosity + specificity
- ✅ "How This Guy Owns 3 Ferraris (We Asked)"
- ✅ "The Billionaire's Car Philosophy - Interview"
- ✅ "$2M Garage Tour | Marcus Hypercar Collection"
- ❌ "Interview #1"
- ❌ "Our Interview with Someone Cool"

**Description:**
- Intro (hook) - 2 sentences about why this matters
- Timestamps - make people jump to moments
- Guest info - name, Instagram, company
- Your info - subscribe, merch link
- Hashtags - #LiveItIconic #LuxuryLifestyle #Cars

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ Over-producing - People want authenticity, not Hollywood
❌ Long pauses/bad audio - Kill the vibe, hurt retention
❌ No structure - Interview needs arc (story → insights → philosophy)
❌ Inconsistent uploads - Algorithm favors consistency
❌ Ignoring comments - Reply to every comment first month
❌ Generic thumbnails - Weird faces and text work best
❌ No value prop - Ask "why should someone watch this?"

## First Interview Checklist

**Before Recording:**
- [ ] Guest has signed release form
- [ ] Location confirmed (test audio/lighting)
- [ ] Battery full on all devices
- [ ] Riverside.fm + backup phone recording ready
- [ ] Talked through 3-4 talking points
- [ ] Camera positioned (eye level)
- [ ] Audio levels tested (not peaking)
- [ ] Frame composition checked (good headroom, background)

**After Recording:**
- [ ] Thank guest and ask for share
- [ ] Get their Instagram/socials
- [ ] Export video + audio + transcript
- [ ] Upload to Riverside or backup storage
- [ ] Start editing within 24 hours
