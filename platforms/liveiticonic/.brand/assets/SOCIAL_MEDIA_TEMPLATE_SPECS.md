## YouTube Templates

### 1. Thumbnail Template

**Dimensions:** 1280 × 720px (16:9 aspect ratio)
**File Format:** PNG or JPG (under 2MB)
**Color Mode:** RGB

**Design Specifications:**

**Layout Option A: Face + Text + Car**
```
┌──────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                              │
│  [Guest Face - Large]    [BOLD TEXT]        │
│  (Left 2/3)              (Right 1/3)        │
│                          [Car Image]         │
│  [Small LII logo]       (Bottom right)      │
│  (Bottom left)                              │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Typography:**
- Headline text: DM Sans Bold, 72-96pt
- Max 3-5 words
- Color: #FFFFFF with black stroke (4pt) OR #D4AF37 (gold)
- Letter spacing: -2%

**Elements:**
- Guest face: Prominent, showing emotion (surprise, joy, pride)
- Car: Visible but secondary (bottom right, 30% of frame)
- Logo: LII monogram, bottom left, 80×80px, semi-transparent

**Color Treatment:**
- High contrast background (dark background, light subject OR vice versa)
- Subtle vignette around edges
- Color grade matches brand (slightly warm, rich blacks)

**Layout Option B: Text + Car Focus**
```
┌──────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                [BOLD TEXT]                   │
│                (Top center)                  │
│                                              │
│         [Car - Large, centered]             │
│                                              │
│  [Small LII logo]                           │
│  (Bottom left)                              │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Typography:** Same as Option A

**Use When:** Guest prefers not to be in thumbnail OR car is exceptionally photogenic

### 3. Instagram Reels

**Dimensions:** 1080 × 1920px (9:16 vertical)
**Duration:** 15-90 seconds
**File Format:** MP4

**Text Overlay Specs:**
- Font: DM Sans Bold
- Size: 72-96pt (readable at small size)
- Color: #FFFFFF with stroke or shadow
- Position: Center or bottom third
- Animate: Fade in, slide up, or pop

**Captions:**
- Always add auto-captions (Instagram's tool or CapCut)
- Style: Yellow or white text, black background
- Position: Center (where face isn't)

**Branding:**
- LII logo watermark: Bottom left or right, 10% opacity, 120×120px

**Dimensions:** 1080 × 1080px per slide (up to 10 slides)

## TikTok Templates

### 1. Standard TikTok Video

**Dimensions:** 1080 × 1920px (9:16)
**Duration:** 15-60 seconds
**File Format:** MP4

**Hook (First 3 Seconds):**
- Text overlay: Large, bold, intriguing
- Example: "He spent $50K on this engine swap..."
- Font: DM Sans Bold, 96pt
- Color: #FFFFFF or #D4AF37

**Body (Middle):**
- Quick cuts (2-3 seconds per shot)
- Sync cuts to music beat
- Add text to emphasize key points

**CTA (Last 3 Seconds):**
```
FULL STORY ON
YOUTUBE
Link in bio →
```

**Captions:**
- Always use auto-captions (TikTok's tool)
- Style: White text, black background OR yellow text

**Trending Elements:**
- Use trending sounds when relevant
- Add hashtags: #carsoftiktok #carporn #automotive #liveiticonic

## Design Tools & Resources

### Recommended Tools

**For Static Graphics:**
- **Canva Pro** (easiest, templates available)
- **Adobe Illustrator** (most control, vector)
- **Figma** (collaborative, free tier available)
- **Affinity Designer** (one-time purchase, no subscription)

**For Video Thumbnails:**
- **Photoshop** (advanced editing)
- **Canva Pro** (quick and easy)
- **Thumbnail Maker** (mobile app)

**For Social Media Videos:**
- **CapCut** (free, mobile and desktop, great for Reels/TikTok)
- **Adobe Premiere Rush** (simplified Premiere)
- **InShot** (mobile app)

### Template Files

**Create Master Templates in:**
- Canva: Shareable templates for all sizes
- Photoshop: PSD files with layers
- Figma: Shareable frames and components

**File Naming Convention:**
```
LII_[Platform]_[Type]_Template_v1.psd

Examples:
LII_YouTube_Thumbnail_Template_v1.psd
LII_Instagram_Story_Template_v1.fig
LII_Email_Header_Template_v1.png
```

## Brand Consistency Checklist

Before publishing ANY social media content, verify:

- [ ] **Colors match brand palette** (#0A0A0A, #D4AF37, #FFFFFF)
- [ ] **Fonts are correct** (DM Sans or Inter only)
- [ ] **Logo is visible** (if applicable)
- [ ] **Image quality is high** (no pixelation, no blur)
- [ ] **Text is readable at small size** (mobile-first)
- [ ] **Tone matches brand voice** (elegant, authentic, not hype)
- [ ] **Aspect ratio is correct** (per platform specs)
- [ ] **File size is within limits** (under platform maximums)

## A/B Testing Guidelines

### What to Test

**Thumbnails:**
- Face vs. no face
- Text placement (left vs. right)
- Color background (dark vs. light)
- Text length (3 words vs. 5 words)

**Instagram Captions:**
- Question vs. statement
- Short (1 sentence) vs. long (3-5 sentences)
- Emoji usage (none vs. 2-3)

**TikTok Hooks:**
- Question hook vs. statement hook
- Text-first vs. visual-first
- Slow reveal vs. immediate payoff

### How to Test

1. **Create 2-3 variations** of the same content
2. **Publish at similar times** (same day of week, same time)
3. **Measure performance** (CTR for thumbnails, engagement for posts)
4. **Analyze after 7 days** (give enough time for data)
5. **Implement winning format** going forward

## Platform-Specific Best Practices

### YouTube
- Thumbnail is 50% of CTR success—invest time here
- Include faces when possible (increases CTR by 20-30%)
- Text should be readable at 320px width (mobile size)

### Instagram
- Maintain consistent visual aesthetic across feed (use same filter/preset)
- Post when audience is most active (check Instagram Insights)
- Engage with comments within first hour of posting (boosts algorithm)

### TikTok
- Hook in first 3 seconds is critical (viewer decides to watch or swipe)
- Use trending sounds to boost discoverability
- Post 1-3 times per day for maximum reach

### Twitter/X
- Text is king—images are supplementary
- Keep tweets concise (under 280 characters is best)
- Engage with replies to boost visibility
