\section{About Live It Iconic}

\subsection{Who We Are}

Live It Iconic is an automotive lifestyle brand that celebrates the stories behind the cars. We're not a review channel. We're not a spec-sheet comparison site. We're a platform for authentic conversations with the people who build, collect, and drive with passion.

\vspace{0.5cm}

\textbf{Our Format:}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
    \item 8-12 minute interview-style videos
    \item Focus on the person, their journey, their "why"
    \item Cinematically shot, elegantly edited
    \item Real stories, not manufactured hype
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Our Audience}

\textbf{Demographics:}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
    \item \textbf{Age:} 25-45 years old (68\% of audience)
    \item \textbf{Gender:} 72\% male, 28\% female
    \item \textbf{Income:} \$50K-150K+ annually
    \item \textbf{Location:} 85\% United States, 10\% Canada, 5\% International
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Psychographics:}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
    \item Car enthusiasts who value \textit{experience} over \textit{specs}
    \item Professionals with disposable income
    \item Quality-conscious consumers (willing to pay for craftsmanship)
    \item Interested in entrepreneurship, design, lifestyle
    \item Followers of brands like Hodinkee, Huckberry, Hagerty
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Brand Values}

\begin{description}[leftmargin=3cm, style=nextline]
    \item[\textcolor{iconicgold}{Authenticity}] We tell real stories, not manufactured hype
    \item[\textcolor{iconicgold}{Elegance}] Premium quality without pretension
    \item[\textcolor{iconicgold}{Community}] You're joining a collective, not buying a logo
    \item[\textcolor{iconicgold}{Passion}] For cars, design, and the lifestyle they represent
    \item[\textcolor{iconicgold}{Precision}] Attention to detail in everything we create
\end{description}

\section{Partnership Opportunities}

\subsection{Why Partner With Us?}

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*]
    \item \textbf{Engaged Audience:} High retention rates (50-60\% avg view duration)
    \item \textbf{Quality-Conscious Viewers:} Audience values craftsmanship and is willing to invest in premium products
    \item \textbf{Authentic Integration:} We only partner with brands we genuinely use and recommend
    \item \textbf{Long-Term Value:} Content library compounds—videos continue driving value for years
    \item \textbf{Multi-Platform Reach:} YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, email newsletter
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Sponsorship Packages}

\subsubsection{Package 1: Video Integration}

\textbf{Investment:} \$2,000-4,000 per video

\textbf{Deliverables:}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
    \item 60-90 second integrated product mention in video
    \item Natural product placement (not forced)
    \item Verbal endorsement from host
    \item Clickable link in video description (pinned comment)
    \item Social media posts (Instagram story + post)
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Best For:} Tool brands, detailing products, insurance, automotive accessories

---

\subsubsection{Package 2: Dedicated Product Review}

\textbf{Investment:} \$4,000-6,000

\textbf{Deliverables:}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
    \item Full 8-12 minute dedicated video reviewing product
    \item Honest, detailed assessment (pros/cons)
    \item B-roll of product in use
    \item Social media promotion across all platforms
    \item Email newsletter feature
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Best For:} Larger products (car care kits, tools, tech products)

---

\subsubsection{Package 3: Multi-Video Campaign}

\textbf{Investment:} \$10,000-15,000

\textbf{Deliverables:}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
    \item Product integration in 3-5 videos over 2-3 months
    \item Recurring brand mentions
    \item Custom discount code tracking for audience
    \item Quarterly performance report
    \item Social media amplification
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Best For:} Brands seeking ongoing presence and brand recall

---

\subsubsection{Package 4: Brand Ambassador Partnership}

\textbf{Investment:} \$25,000-50,000 annually

\textbf{Deliverables:}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
    \item Exclusive category partnership (no competitors featured)
    \item Logo placement in video outros
    \item Mentions in 12-24 videos annually
    \item Co-branded content opportunities
    \item Event representation
    \item Affiliate commission structure
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Best For:} Brands wanting exclusive, long-term alignment
