## Inventory Management

### Inventory Tracking System

**Tool:** Google Sheets or Shopify inventory (for real-time tracking)

**Columns:**
- SKU
- Product Name
- Size
- Quantity on Hand
- Quantity Reserved (pending orders)
- Quantity Available
- Reorder Point
- Supplier
- Last Restock Date

### Minimum Stock Levels

Set reorder points based on sales velocity:

| Product | Minimum Stock (per size) | Reorder Quantity |
|---------|--------------------------|------------------|
| T-Shirt (each size) | 10 units | 25 units |
| Hoodie (each size) | 5 units | 15 units |
| Cap | 15 units | 30 units |

### Reorder Process

**Trigger:** When inventory hits minimum stock level

**Process:**

1. **Check Sales Velocity**
   - Review last 30 days of sales
   - Adjust reorder quantity if demand has changed

2. **Contact Supplier**
   - Email purchase order (PO)
   - Include: SKU, quantity, desired delivery date
   - Request confirmation within 24 hours

3. **Track Order**
   - Log PO in tracking spreadsheet
   - Follow up if no confirmation within 48 hours
   - Update expected delivery date

4. **Receive & Inspect**
   - Count all items upon arrival
   - Inspect quality (see Quality Control section)
   - Update inventory system immediately

## Order Processing

### Order Flow (E-Commerce)

**Platform:** Shopify or WooCommerce

**Automated Process:**

1. **Customer Places Order**
   - Receives immediate confirmation email
   - Order appears in admin panel

2. **Order Review (Within 24 Hours)**
   - Check for fraud (mismatched billing/shipping, unusual orders)
   - Verify inventory availability
   - Flag any issues (out of stock, address problems)

3. **Order Fulfillment (Within 48 Hours)**
   - Pick items from inventory
   - Conduct quality check
   - Package according to standards
   - Generate shipping label
   - Update order status to "Shipped"
   - Customer receives tracking email automatically

### Manual Order Entry (For Events/Direct Sales)

**Process:**

1. Record customer info: Name, email, shipping address
2. Create order manually in Shopify/system
3. Collect payment (Square, Stripe, cash)
4. Issue receipt
5. Fulfill same as e-commerce orders

## Quality Control

### Pre-Ship Inspection Checklist

For EVERY item before packaging:

- [ ] **Check for Defects**
  - No stains, marks, or discoloration
  - No loose threads or stitching errors
  - No holes or tears

- [ ] **Verify Logo Placement**
  - Embroidery is clean and centered
  - No loose stitching on logo
  - Color is correct (should be consistent)

- [ ] **Check Size & SKU**
  - Tag matches ordered size
  - Correct product for order

- [ ] **Packaging Condition**
  - No wrinkles (steam or fold neatly)
  - Fresh, clean smell (no warehouse odor)

**If Item Fails QC:**
- Remove from inventory
- Mark as defective in system
- Contact supplier for replacement or refund
- Do NOT ship to customer

## Packaging Standards

### Packaging Materials

**Required Materials:**

1. **Primary Packaging:**
   - Black matte poly mailers (10x13" for tees/hoodies, 6x9" for caps)
   - OR Black matte boxes for premium orders (multiple items)

2. **Secondary Packaging:**
   - White tissue paper (acid-free)
   - Subtle gold monogram pattern (custom printed or stamp)

3. **Branding Elements:**
   - Thank you card (business card size)
   - Sticker (gold monogram on black or white)
   - Hang tag (if not already attached to product)

4. **Shipping Label:**
   - Printed label (not handwritten)
   - Placed on poly mailer or box

### Packaging Process

**Step-by-Step:**

1. **Prepare Workspace**
   - Clean, flat surface
   - All materials within reach
   - Order slip printed

2. **Fold Item (If Applicable)**
   - T-Shirt: Fold in thirds, then in half
   - Hoodie: Fold sleeves in, fold in half, then thirds
   - Cap: No folding (place in smaller mailer as-is)

3. **Wrap in Tissue Paper**
   - Lay tissue paper flat
   - Place folded item in center
   - Wrap like a gift (neat and tight)
   - Secure with small piece of tape or sticker

4. **Add Branding Elements**
   - Place thank you card on top
   - Add 1-2 stickers
   - Include any promotional inserts (discount codes, etc.)

5. **Place in Mailer/Box**
   - Slide wrapped item into mailer
   - Ensure it fits snugly (not too tight, not too loose)
   - Seal mailer

6. **Apply Shipping Label**
   - Place label on flat side (not seam)
   - Smooth out any bubbles

7. **Final Check**
   - Confirm address is correct
   - Verify order number matches packing slip

## Shipping Procedures

### Shipping Options

**Domestic (US):**

| Service | Speed | Cost (Estimate) | Use For |
|---------|-------|-----------------|---------|
| USPS First Class | 3-5 days | $4-6 | Single items (under 1 lb) |
| USPS Priority Mail | 2-3 days | $8-12 | Multiple items |
| UPS Ground | 3-7 days | $8-15 | Larger orders |

**International:**

| Service | Speed | Cost (Estimate) | Use For |
|---------|-------|-----------------|---------|
| USPS First Class Int'l | 7-21 days | $15-25 | Single items |
| USPS Priority Mail Int'l | 6-10 days | $30-50 | Multiple items |

### Shipping Rates for Customers

**Offer:**
- Free shipping on orders $75+ (absorb cost)
- Flat rate $5 for orders under $75 (covers most First Class)
- International: Calculate at checkout (pass actual cost to customer)

### Daily Shipping Routine

**End-of-Day Process:**

1. **Batch Print Labels**
   - Export all "ready to ship" orders from Shopify
   - Print labels in batch via Shippo, Shopify Shipping, or Stamps.com

2. **Scan & Drop Off**
   - Scan all packages (tracking activated)
   - Drop at USPS or schedule pickup (if daily volume > 10 packages)

3. **Update Orders**
   - Mark orders as shipped in system
   - Customers auto-notified with tracking info

## Returns & Exchanges

### Return Policy

**Customer-Facing Policy:**

- 30-day return window from delivery date
- Items must be unworn, unwashed, with tags attached
- Original packaging not required (but preferred)
- Free returns via prepaid label
- Refund processed within 5-7 business days of receiving return

### Return Process

**Step 1: Customer Initiates Return**

- Customer emails hello@liveiticonic.com with order number and reason
- We respond within 24 hours with prepaid return label

**Step 2: Customer Ships Item**

- Customer prints label, packages item, drops at post office
- Tracking info allows us to monitor return shipment

**Step 3: Receive & Inspect Return**

- [ ] Open package
- [ ] Verify item matches order
- [ ] Inspect condition (unworn, clean, tags attached)
- [ ] If acceptable: Process refund
- [ ] If not acceptable: Contact customer with photos, offer resolution

**Step 4: Process Refund**

- Issue refund to original payment method
- Email customer: "Your return has been received and refund processed. Please allow 3-5 business days for it to appear in your account."

### Exchanges

**Process:**

- Treat as return + new order
- Issue refund for original order
- Customer places new order for correct item
- Consider offering discount code for the inconvenience (10% off)
