## ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes

**DON'T:**
- Overthink the first video (good enough beats perfect)
- Wait for perfect equipment (phone camera is fine)
- Post sporadically (consistency matters more)
- Only promote yourself (build community first)
- Sell products before brand trust (hard to convert)
- Compare to established creators (they took years)

**DO:**
- Start with people you know (lower pressure)
- Keep videos tight (20-25 min is ideal)
- Engage with every comment (you're building a community)
- Be authentic (people smell bullshit)
- Have fun (if you're not enjoying this, it shows)

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: Do I need fancy equipment?**
A: No. iPhone + good lighting + good mic. That's it. Start there.

**Q: How long should videos be?**
A: YouTube interviews: 20-25 min. TikTok/Reels: 60 sec max. Website: varies.

**Q: Should I do daily posts?**
A: No. 2-3x per week is sustainable and Google/TikTok rewards consistency over volume.

**Q: When should I ask about sponsorships?**
A: Month 6+. Get 5K+ followers first.

**Q: What if nobody watches?**
A: Weeks 1-2 are always quiet. By week 4 you'll know if it's resonating. If not, adjust the content, not the brand.

**Q: Do I need to hire an editor?**
A: Month 1-2, you edit. Month 3+, consider outsourcing (costs ~$20-50 per video).

**Q: What's the deal with Twitch?**
A: Optional but recommended. Stream the interviews live on Twitch, then upload edited version to YouTube. Builds community faster.

**Q: When do I make money?**
A: Month 1-3 is investment. Month 3-6 is when you start seeing real revenue from products + ads.

## üé¨ First Interview Script (Keep it Simple)

**Intro (2 min):**
"Hey everyone, I'm [name]. This is Live It Iconic, where we talk to interesting people about the automotive lifestyle and what iconic really means. Today I'm talking to [guest name]..."

**Main (15-20 min):**
"So [guest], tell us... how did you get into [cars/this lifestyle]?"
(Ask 3-5 follow-up questions. Listen more than you talk.)

**Wrap (3 min):**
"Before we go, I want to show you something..."
(Hold up one Live It Iconic product)
"We make these for people who actually live the lifestyle..."
"If you want one, link is in the description"

**Outro (1 min):**
"Thanks [guest] for coming. If you liked this, subscribe, that helps us a lot."
(End with energy, smile)
