## üìê Logo Usage Guidelines

1. **Minimum Size:** Logos should never be smaller than 24px in height
2. **Clear Space:** Maintain padding equal to logo height on all sides
3. **Color Variations:** Each logo has full-color, monochrome, and inverted versions
4. **Background Contrast:** Ensure sufficient contrast (WCAG AA minimum)
